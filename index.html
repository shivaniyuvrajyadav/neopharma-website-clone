who are we ?

Neopharma is a company based in Maputo, Mozambique. The main business is based on the import and distribution of specialized products for the health area, which includes pharmaceutical products, medical/surgical devices, hospital equipment , among others in its portfolio.
The products and services that our company offers are aimed at private clinics, NGOs, government hospitals, pharmacies and large-scale distribution, which comply with the standards of good market practice, as well as duly certified companies.
